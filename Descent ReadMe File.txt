--The Descent -- Instructions --
--How to Play--

Left/Right Arrow Keys to Move
W - Jump
F - Shoot
Enter - Interact

----------------
At the end of each level there will be a choice.
Depending on the choice, you will face either an EASY or HARD level


HARD Levels are designed to be unfair and difficult. So you better
hope to pick the correct level and if you don't, you better hope you're
vigilant in avoiding the traps and completing the task.
